The Story Behind the Evolution

TransparentBusiness was founded on a simple principle: global visibility for efficient governance. As we achieved significant milestones, including over $100M raised and $3B in real estate swaps, we also encountered the unprecedented challenges of an evolving regulatory landscape.

From being targeted by the SEC to navigating the "War on Crypto," the path forward required more than resilience—it demanded evolution. The transition from Unicoin to TBIZ is not just a name change; it is a strategic pivot to safeguard thousands of investors and position ourselves for a planned NYSE listing.

What was at stake was the future of transparent finance. Our evolution ensures that our vision remains intact while our structure aligns with the highest institutional standards.

The War on Innovation

Regulatory pressure has reached unprecedented levels, impacting thousands of global investors.

The current regulatory environment, marked by aggressive positioning from the SEC and leadership like Gary Gensler, has created a climate of uncertainty for digital asset innovators. TransparentBusiness has stood at the front lines of this conflict.

Rather than yielding to speculative framing, we choose transparency. Our response to this "War on Innovation" is a commitment to institutional maturity and legal clarity.
